25 Hadith Collection

(Arabic + English + Proof)

إِنَّمَا الأَعْمَالُ بِالنِّيَّاتِ
Actions are judged by intentions.
(Sahih al-Bukhari & Sahih Muslim)
الدين النصيحة
The religion is sincere advice.
(Sahih Muslim)
من حسن إسلام المرء تركه ما لا يعنيه
Part of a person’s good Islam is leaving what does not concern him.
(Sunan al-Tirmidhi)
لا يؤمن أحدكم حتى يحب لأخيه ما يحب لنفسه
None of you truly believes until he loves for his brother what he loves for himself.
(Sahih al-Bukhari & Sahih Muslim)
من كان يؤمن بالله واليوم الآخر فليقل خيرا أو ليصمت
Whoever believes in Allah and the Last Day should speak good or remain silent.
(Sahih al-Bukhari & Sahih Muslim)
الطهور شطر الإيمان
Purity is half of faith.
(Sahih Muslim)
الدعاء هو العبادة
Supplication is worship.
(Sunan Abu Dawood, Jami` al-Tirmidhi)
الرفق لا يكون في شيء إلا زانه
Gentleness is not in anything except that it beautifies it.
(Sahih Muslim)
المؤمن مرآة المؤمن
A believer is the mirror of another believer.
(Sunan Abu Dawood)
اتق الله حيثما كنت
Fear Allah wherever you are.
(Jami` al-Tirmidhi)
الكلمة الطيبة صدقة
A good word is charity.
(Sahih al-Bukhari & Sahih Muslim)
يسروا ولا تعسروا
Make things easy, and do not make them difficult.
(Sahih al-Bukhari & Sahih Muslim)
إن الله لا ينظر إلى صوركم ولكن إلى قلوبكم
Allah does not look at your forms or appearance but looks at your hearts.
(Sahih Muslim)
المسلم من سلم المسلمون من لسانه ويده
A Muslim is one from whose tongue and hand other Muslims are safe.
(Sahih al-Bukhari & Sahih Muslim)
من صمت نجا
Whoever remains silent is saved.
(Sunan al-Tirmidhi)
أفضل الجهاد كلمة حق عند سلطان جائر
The best jihad is a word of truth before a tyrant ruler.
(Sunan Abu Dawood)
المؤمن القوي خير وأحب إلى الله من المؤمن الضعيف
The strong believer is better and more beloved to Allah than the weak believer.
(Sahih Muslim)
تبسمك في وجه أخيك صدقة
Your smile for your brother is charity.
(Jami` al-Tirmidhi)
خيركم من تعلم القرآن وعلمه
The best of you are those who learn the Qur’an and teach it.
(Sahih al-Bukhari)
من صلى البردين دخل الجنة
Whoever prays the two cool prayers (Fajr and Asr) will enter Paradise.
(Sahih al-Bukhari & Sahih Muslim)
الصوم جنة
Fasting is a shield.
(Sahih al-Bukhari & Sahih Muslim)
الحج عرفة
Hajj is (standing at) Arafah.
(Sunan al-Tirmidhi)
من لا يشكر الناس لا يشكر الله
He who does not thank people, does not thank Allah.
(Musnad Ahmad, Sunan al-Tirmidhi)
سيد الاستغفار أن يقول العبد...
The best way of seeking forgiveness is that a servant says: "O Allah, You are my Lord..."
(Sahih al-Bukhari)
من غشنا فليس منا
Whoever cheats us is not one of us.
(Sahih Muslim)
